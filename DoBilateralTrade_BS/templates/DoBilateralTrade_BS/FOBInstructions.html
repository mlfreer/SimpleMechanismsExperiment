The exact chance of winning is computed as follows:
  • We take all submitted prices of the players which played the different role from you from this session.

  • We compute the share of observation which are below and above of your submitted belief (median).
    That is if there are 70 observations below your submitted beliefs and 30 above, then the relative shares would be .7 and .3 (just divided numbers by 100, that is the total number of observations).
  • The (percentage) chance of winning is equal to the product of these relative shares multiplied by 400.

By the chance of winning we mean the that the computer would generate a random number between 0 and 100 (inclusively with all numbers having same chance of being chose) and if the number is below your chance of winning then you are getting the prize, otherwise you are not getting the prize.
    Note that if the your report is such that exactly half of the observations are below and half are above, then you are getting the prize for sure.
